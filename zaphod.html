• Zaphod Beeblebrox



    Zaphod Beeblebrox was born in a planet in the vicinity of Betelgeuse and shares three of the same mothers as Ford Prefect, thus being his semi-half-cousin. He is the President of the Galaxy (full title President of the Imperial Galactic Government), and the inventor of the Pan-Galactic Gargle Blaster, the best drink in existence, whose effects are described by the Guide as ‘similar to having your brains smashed in by a slice of lemon wrapped round a large gold brick’.

  • Sam Rockwell



    Sam Rockwell was born in California in 1968 and has participated in a number of Hollywood productions, as well as numerous indie films. His career includes roles in Moon, Confessions of a Dangerous Mind, Matchstick Men, Seven Psychopaths, The Assassination of Jesse James by the Coward Robert Ford, Frost/Nixon and Iron Man 2.
