• Tricia McMillan



    Tricia McMillan is a brilliant mathematician and astrophysicist whom Arthur Dent talked to at a party in Islington. She leaves the Earth with Zaphod Beeblebrox and travels with him on the spaceship Heart of Gold, where she and Arthur next meet, shortly after the Earth has been destroyed to make way for a hyperspace bypass. She later changes her name to Trillian, because she feels it is more ‘space-y’.

  • Zooey Deschanel



    Zooey Deschanel was born in California in 1980 and is famous for her musical career, as well as her acting roles. These include participations in movies such as Almost Famous, Elf, Yes Man, Bridge to Terabithia, and (500) Days of Summer, in which she plays the main female character. She is also well known for her role as Jessica Day on the award-winning TV show New Girl.
